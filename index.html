The Team

Mind Stream Divisions

IT

IT Division was established in 2003 and have since been operating in Saudi as a solution provider, MS-IT have executed several key projects in Saudi Arabia involving integrating several technologies and applications. As another step towards bringing strong partnerships to Saudi , A partnership and representation in 2006 was signed with ENC - Egyptian Networking Consultancy Company to execute projects both in Saudi and Egypt, MindStream and ENC strengthened the relation with ENC further by using MindStream contact base to get several key projects for ENC in Saudi Arabia.

Retail

MindStream entered the retailing market by acquiring shares in several retail operations in different fields such as toys, electronics, textiles, and clothing.

Entertainment

MindStream established the entertainment and events division in 2017 to become partner with several agencies from Europe, USA and Canada as this is a growing business sector in Saudi Arabia. This year several events focused on children and family, sports, and other business-oriented events will be launched in Saudi Arabia.

Hajj - Umrah

MindStream Hajj and Umrah division was established in 2018 as a new divison focused on providing top services for Hajj and Umrah and will be launched in lodging, transportation and catering services.

Construction

MindStream Construction and building materials started in 2007, since then it have implemented several projects of several types residential and commercial.

Industrial

About Mind Stream

MindStream was established in 2003 by Eng. Ahmed Al-Kouther in the area of Information Technology, and since have grown in several business areas in Saudi Arabia, UAE and Egypt.

With about 35 Years experience in the Saudi market and coming from a family heritage of business background in several sectors in the market, having the right organization, Best location, well educated and quality staff and complementing all this with an extensive contact base in many governmental and private sectors.

MindStream Have successfully established several partnerships and dealerships with several Arab and international companies and is continuing to grow with steady, well-planned, well- managed performance.
